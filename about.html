About Us

Our Story

Racetrac Gas Station & Liquor Store has been serving the Creston and Erickson communities for years. What started as a small roadside fueling stop has grown into a full‑service station offering premium fuels, a diverse liquor selection and a well‑stocked convenience store. We’re family owned and operated, proud to be a part of this beautiful region and committed to delivering exceptional service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide travelers and locals alike with the essentials they need, whether that’s a full tank of fuel, a bottle of wine for a special dinner or a quick snack on the go. We strive to be friendly, efficient and always welcoming.

Why Choose Us?

  • Competitive fuel prices and high‑quality products
  • Wide selection of beer, wine and spirits
  • Clean and modern facilities
  • Convenient location along BC‑3
  • Friendly staff ready to assist
